TCP = Tranmission Control Protocol 

UDP = User Datagram Protocol 

UDP socket doesn't keep track of the order that the packets are sent in. 
Given that the over head is less UDP is bested used for video streaming, multi player video games. Also UDP is used by DNS. 

TCP socket is used for many protocols like HTTP, FTP, SSH, and SMTP. 
TCP numbers the packets so that its always in order and prevents the resending of packets.
